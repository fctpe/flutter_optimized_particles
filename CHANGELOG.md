  * RippleEffectWidget
  * ShockwaveWidget
  * ProjectileWidget
  * PulseGlowWidget
  * ImpactFlashWidget
  * FloatingFeedbackWidget
* Battery optimization with automatic background pause
* Zero setState() calls for better performance
